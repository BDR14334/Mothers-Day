Happy Mother's Day, Mom!

Isaiah 66:13

Mom,

I just want to take a moment to say how much I love you, Mom, and to thank you for everything you’ve done and continue to do for me. From the time I was a toddler to now as an adult, you have always been there supporting me through every phase, no matter what state I was in. Whether I was cheerful and bubbly or going through a rough patch, your love never wavered.

I’m incredibly grateful for your patience, your strength, and your unwavering kindness. Every sacrifice you’ve made big or small has never gone unnoticed, even if I haven’t always said it out loud. You’ve given so much of yourself to help shape who I am, and I’ll forever be thankful for that.

As you enter this next chapter in your life, I truly hope it brings you joy, growth, and even greater opportunities. Keep putting your heart into your craft the way you’ve always poured your heart into raising and supporting me. I can’t wait to see where this path takes you because you deserve it.

Your Son, Brandon

I love you so much, Mom. I hope you have a beautiful day.❤️
